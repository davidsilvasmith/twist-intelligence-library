Why this exists

A searchable memory for the venture ecosystem.

TWiST runs a daily interview with operators building at the edge. The conversations are rich, but the insights evaporate. The Library compiles each episode into a persistent, linkable form: theses, axioms, decision rules, and a graph of the people and companies behind them.
