# E2266 - 32 Million Dollar Fraud? Delve Scandal

## 5-minute masterclass In this episode, Jason Calacanis and Alex Wilhelm investigate explosive fraud allegations against Delve, a YC-backed compliance startup that raised $32M. They discuss whistleblower claims of fabricated audit evidence, 'rubber-stamp' certifications, and the use of offshore 'mills' instead of advertised CPA firms. The panel explores the systemic risks for Delve’s 1,500+ clients, the failure of investor due diligence during the AI gold rush, and the broader implications of 'fake it till you make it' culture in highly regulated security sectors.
