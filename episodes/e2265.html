# E2265 - The Drone Company Everyone Thought Was Illegal (Now Worth $4B+)

## 5-minute masterclass Keller Rinaudo Cliffton, CEO of Zipline, discusses building the world's largest commercial autonomous delivery system. Faced with U.S. regulatory barriers, Zipline launched in Rwanda, proving its life-saving impact by slashing maternal mortality. Now valued at $4B+, the company is expanding into U.S. home delivery with its advanced P2 technology. The episode also features Superhuman founder Rahul Vohra, who explores the psychology of 'difficult' founders and the pursuit of product-market fit in competitive software categories.
