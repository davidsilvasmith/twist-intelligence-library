# E2263 - Are Brain-Computer Interfaces Actually Ready for Humans?

## 5-minute masterclass Jason Calacanis explores the frontiers of deep tech with Paradromics CEO Matt Angle, discussing their high-density BCI's move toward human clinical trials for restoring speech and motor function. The conversation compares BCI approaches to Neuralink while highlighting therapeutic potential for mental health. The episode further investigates sustainable aviation with JetZero's blended wing designs and the evolution of proactive wellness through Nutrisense's real-time metabolic tracking, illustrating a broader shift toward integrating sophisticated hardware with human biology and transportation for radical efficiency and health optimization.
