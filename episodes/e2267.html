# E2267 - This Bittensor Subnet Could Cut Drug Discovery Costs in HALF

## 5-minute masterclass Micaela Bazo, CEO of Metanova Labs, discusses Bittensor Subnet 68 (NOVA), a decentralized AI network accelerating drug discovery. By crowdsourcing the screening of 65 billion molecules and incentivizing miners to optimize search algorithms, NOVA aims to slash drug development costs by up to 50%. The episode explores how decentralized virtual screening outperforms traditional centralized models, leveraging adversarial mining to refine predictive accuracy for identifying potential drug candidates.
