# E2270 - Venture Roundtable: SpaceX IPO, AI's PR Crisis, and the Defense Tech Bubble

## 5-minute masterclass Jason Calacanis and Alex Wilhelm host a Venture Roundtable with Delian Asparouhov, Salen Churi, and Larsen Jensen to analyze SpaceX's $1.75 trillion confidential IPO filing. The panel explores the widening gap between Silicon Valley's AI optimism and public anxiety over job loss. They also debate the sustainability of the $49 billion defense tech sector, the strategic necessity of reindustrializing American supply chains, and Marc Andreessen's 'zero introspection' thesis for elite founders navigating high-stakes growth.
