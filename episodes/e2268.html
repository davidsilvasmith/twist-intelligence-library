# E2268 - The $60 billion resource hiding in space, and the startup trying to mine it

## 5-minute masterclass Jason Calacanis interviews AstroForge CEO Matt Gialich about mining platinum group metals from near-Earth asteroids. AstroForge uses directed energy and magnetism to extract resources, aiming for 90% gross margins while reducing terrestrial mining's environmental impact. The episode also features Sam Dare of Templar discussing decentralized AI model training on blockchain and Yazin Alirhayim of OpenOats presenting an open-source alternative for AI meeting notes. They explore the future of space-based resource extraction and decentralized AI infrastructure.
